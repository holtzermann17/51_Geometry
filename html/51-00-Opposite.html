opposite


Given a right triangle with an acute angle θ, the side of the triangle that is opposite of θ is the side of the triangle that is not a side (http://planetmath.org/Angle) of θ.

This definition can be generalized to any triangle and any angle θ: The side of the triangle that is opposite of θ is the side of the triangle that is not a side of θ. This more general definition is not used as commonly as the definition for right triangles.
