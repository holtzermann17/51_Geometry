lengths of triangle medians


By the Apollonius theorem, in any triangle, the ma, mb, mc of the medians (http://planetmath.org/Median) of opposing the the sides a, b, c, respectively, are

ma=12⁢2⁢b2+2⁢c2-a2,
mb=12⁢2⁢c2+2⁢a2-b2,
mc=12⁢2⁢a2+2⁢b2-c2.
