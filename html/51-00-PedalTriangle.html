pedal triangle


The pedal triangle of any triangle △⁢A⁢B⁢C, is the triangle whose vertices are the feet of perpendiculars from A, B and C to their opposite sides in △⁢A⁢B⁢C.

In this figure, the △⁢D⁢E⁢F is the pedal triangle of △⁢A⁢B⁢C.

In general, for any point P inside a triangle, the pedal triangle of P is a triangle whose vertices are the feet of perpendiculars from P to the sides of the triangle.
