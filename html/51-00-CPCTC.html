CPCTC


“CPCTC” is an acronym which stands for “corresponding parts of congruent (http://planetmath.org/Congruent2) triangles (http://planetmath.org/Triangle) are congruent.” In other , if

△⁢A⁢B⁢C≅△⁢D⁢E⁢F, (1)

then all of the following are true:

  • •

    ∠⁢A≅∠⁢D;

  • •

    A⁢B¯≅D⁢E¯;

  • •

    ∠⁢B≅∠⁢E;

  • •

    B⁢C¯≅E⁢F¯;

  • •

    ∠⁢C≅∠⁢F;

  • •

    A⁢C¯≅D⁢F¯.

Note.  The notation in (1) indicates that the isomorphism in question maps A↦D,  B↦E,  and  C↦F.
