linear ordered geometry


An incidence geometry A=(P,n,I) is a linear ordered geometry if there is a strict betweenness relation B defined on the points P0 of A, such that

  • Col1

    (p,q,r)∈B only if p,q, and r are collinear (all incident with a common line ℓ∈P1);

  • Col2

    for any pairwise distinct collinear points p,q,r, at least one of (p,q,r), (q,r,p), or (r,p,q)∈B,

We denote the linear ordered geometry by (A,B).
