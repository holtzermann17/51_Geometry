construction of contraharmonic mean of two segments


Let a and b two line segments (and their ).  The contraharmonic mean

x:=a2+b2a+b=(a2+b2)2a+b,

satisfying the proportion equation

a+ba2+b2=a2+b2x,

can be constructed geometrically (http://planetmath.org/GeometricConstruction) as the third proportional of the segments a+b and a2+b2, the latter of which is gotten as the hypotenuse of the right triangle with catheti a and b.  See the construction of fourth proportional.

(The dotted lines are parallel, with declivity 45∘.)
