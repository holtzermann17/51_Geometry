Stewart’s theorem


Let be given a triangle △⁢A⁢B⁢C with A⁢B=c, B⁢C=a, C⁢A=b, and a point X on B⁢C such that B⁢X=m and X⁢C=n. Denote with p the length of A⁢X.

Then

a⁢(p2+m⁢n)=b2⁢m+c2⁢n.
