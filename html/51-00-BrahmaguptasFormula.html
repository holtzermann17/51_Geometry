Brahmagupta’s formula


If a cyclic quadrilateral has sides p,q,r,s then its area is given by

(T-p)⁢(T-q)⁢(T-r)⁢(T-s)

where T=p+q+r+s2.

Note that if s→0, Heron’s formula is recovered.
