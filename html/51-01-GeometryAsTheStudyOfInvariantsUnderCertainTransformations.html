0.1.4 Spherical geometry

Spherical geometry deals with restricting our attention in Euclidean space to the unit sphere Sn. The role of straight lines is taken by great circles. Notions of distance and angles can be easily developed, as well as spherical laws of cosines, the law of sines, and spherical triangles.
