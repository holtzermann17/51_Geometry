Brunn-Minkowski inequality


Let A and B be non-empty compact subsets of ℝd. Then

vol(A+B)1/d≥vol(A)1/d+vol(B)1/d,

where A+B denotes the Minkowski sum of A and B, and vol⁡(S) denotes the volume of S.
