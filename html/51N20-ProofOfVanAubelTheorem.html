proof of Van Aubel theorem


We want to prove

C⁢PP⁢F=C⁢DD⁢B+C⁢EE⁢A

On the picture, let us call ϕ to the angle ∠⁢A⁢B⁢E and ψ to the angle ∠⁢E⁢B⁢C.

A generalization of bisector’s theorem states

C⁢EE⁢A=C⁢B⁢sin⁡ψA⁢B⁢sin⁡ϕ on ⁢△⁢A⁢B⁢C

and

C⁢PP⁢F=C⁢B⁢sin⁡ψF⁢B⁢sin⁡ϕ on ⁢△⁢F⁢B⁢C.

From the two equalities we can get

C⁢E⋅A⁢BE⁢A=C⁢P⋅F⁢BP⁢F

and thus

C⁢PP⁢F=C⁢E⋅A⁢BE⁢A⋅F⁢B.

Since A⁢B=A⁢F+F⁢B, substituting leads to

C⁢E⋅A⁢BE⁢A⋅F⁢B =C⁢E⁢(A⁢F+F⁢B)E⁢A⋅F⁢B
=C⁢E⋅A⁢FE⁢A⋅F⁢B+C⁢E⋅F⁢BE⁢A⋅F⁢B
=C⁢E⋅A⁢FE⁢A⋅F⁢B+C⁢EE⁢A

But Ceva’s theorem states

C⁢EE⁢A⋅A⁢FF⁢B⋅B⁢DD⁢C=1

and so

C⁢E⋅A⁢FE⁢A⋅F⁢B=C⁢DD⁢B

Subsituting the last equality gives the desired result.
