coordinate way


Let  P=(x,y,z)  be a point in ℝ3. If N is the projection (http://planetmath.org/ProjectionOfPoint) of P on the x⁢y-plane, A the projection of N on the x-axis and O the origin, then the broken line  P⁢N⁢A⁢O  is the coordinate way of the point P. The lengths of the line segments forming the coordinate way are

A⁢O=|x|,N⁢A=|y|,P⁢N=|z|.
