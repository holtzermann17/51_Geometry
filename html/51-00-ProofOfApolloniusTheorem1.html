proof of Apollonius theorem


Let m be a median of the triangle, as shown in the figure.

By Stewart’s theorem we have

a⁢(m2+(a2)2)=b2⁢(a2)+c2⁢(a2)

and thus

m2+(a2)2=b2+c22.

Multiplying both sides by 2 gives

2⁢m2+a22=b2+c2.

QED
