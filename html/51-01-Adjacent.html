adjacent


Given a right triangle with an acute angle θ, the side of the triangle that is adjacent to θ is the side of the triangle that is also a side (http://planetmath.org/Angle) of θ and is not the hypotenuse.

When a phrase such as “adjacent of an angle” is used, one must determine from context whether it refers to this definition of adjacent or the other definition of adjacent (http://planetmath.org/Adjacent3). Note that the definition supplied above is specifically for right triangles.
