Rodrigues’ rotation formula


Rodrigues’ rotation formula gives a convenient way to write the general rotation matrix in R3.

If [v1,v2,v3] is a unit vector on the rotation axis, and θ is the rotation angle about that axis, then the rotation matrix is given by

I+sin⁡(θ)⁢A+(1-cos⁡(θ))⁢A2

where I is the identity matrix and

A=(0-v3v2v30-v1-v2v10)

.
